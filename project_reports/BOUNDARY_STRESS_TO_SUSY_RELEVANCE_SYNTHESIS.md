# Boundary Stress To SUSY Relevance Synthesis

Date: 2026-06-09

The real-data fitted N-Frame boundary equation was frozen and applied to available local SUSY-like benchmark samples. This asks whether benchmark signals occupy high real-data B_NF tails.

The current pilot is incomplete for SUSY specificity because no local SM simulated backgrounds were found. Therefore it cannot yet establish that SUSY-like benchmarks are more high-boundary than ttbar or QCD backgrounds.

This is not a discovery claim and not evidence that SUSY was found. The result is best treated as a benchmark-level pilot and a preparation step for a proper SUSY-vs-SM specificity test.
